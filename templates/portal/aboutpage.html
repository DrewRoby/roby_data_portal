Hello! Please feel free to poke around here and explore, but be warned that this site is under construction at all times.

What this means is that there will be (ARE!) broken pieces of each application. I'm using this project to practice full-stack web design with Django, and (hey presto!) it's not an entirely straightforward process.

So why, I hear you ask, am I implementing Tech Debt As A Feature (TDAAF)? Simple: because bug tracking and resolution are part of the process too. Too many ideas, too little time. Can't implement everything perfectly or cleanly on the first go-round, and this is primarily an educational effort. In future, when the site formally launches, this will not be the case

But for now, in the words of the great William Smith, "It's the Wild Wild West. Jim West, Desperado, Rough Rider, no you don't want nada, brother running this, six-gunning this" &c. &c.

The goal right now is not to leave pages or links absolutely broken. If you come across something that isn't working, please report it in the repo using the Bug Report link (message me for repo access, andrew at ~thisdomain~ dot com)
